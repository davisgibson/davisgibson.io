Reflection:

Our first goal was to make a calculator by using gui and drawpad. After further research, we decided that that would be a little too much for us. We decided to make a simple calculator, but with many added functions. I thought it was well structured in my mind, in the sense that it used functions effectively and has many uses. I thought the hardest part was adding a while loop to the adding and averaging functions, because I encountered many problems with changing variables. It was all worth it though, we ended up with an adding function that could theoretically take infinite inputs and add/average them together. The other functions were relatively easy, even the pythagorean theorem function.
